A palindrome is a word or phrase spelled the same forwards as it is backwards once all non-alphanumeric characters are removed and all letters are set to the same case. For instance, "tacocat", "racecar", and "eye" are palindromes. In this version "_eye" or "_?&taco_&cAt_*" are also palindromes, whereas "camel" is NOT.
